Privacy Policy

YouTube Time Extension values your privacy. This extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with any server or third party.

What Data is Stored?

  • Settings: Your theme, color, and display preferences are stored locally in your browser's extension storage.
  • Watch Statistics: Your YouTube watch time and related statistics are stored locally in your browser's extension storage.

Where is Data Stored?

  • Data is stored using your browser's storage.sync, storage.local, and localStorage APIs. This means your data stays on your device and, if you are signed in and sync is enabled, may be synced across your devices via your browser's built-in sync feature.

How is Data Used?

  • Data is used solely to provide extension functionality, such as displaying your watch time statistics and saving your preferences.
  • No data is sent to any external server or third party.

How Can I Control My Data?

  • You can export, import, or clear your data at any time from the extension's options page.
  • Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns, please contact the extension author via the support page or repository.
